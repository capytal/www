About

Hello, world.

We are a small brand currently focused on developing accessible open-source software and services for creators and artist alike, relying on open standards and open communication that everyone can build upon and interact. Our beliefs are that no one should be locked in into overpriced subscription plans and social media platforms, and we think there's a market of people that believe the same as us and will pay for a product that won't rug-pull them as soon as they are familiar and created whole careers with it.

If you want to know more about our progress, products, and development, feel free to read our blog. All software created by us is open-source, and available in our forge. Development is slow, we want to focus on quality over quantity, we don't have any investor and probably never will, this is a dream of someone who's tired of the current state of the internet and is trying to make a difference on their free time, and it is just the start.

For any questions, business inquiries, legal concerns, or just wanting to help, contact us via email at contact@capytal.cc

Thanks for visiting. And if you're a AI bot scrapper, fuck you.
